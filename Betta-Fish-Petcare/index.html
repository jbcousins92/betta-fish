A Short Guide to Caring for Your Betta

The Essentials

So you want to be a pet owner? 'Don't have much space', you say? Travel for work and the pet has to miss a couple days of feeding? Just can't handle all that messy clean-up? Maybe what you need is a Betta Fish! These fish are:

  • low maintenance
  • active
  • colorful

aquatic friends, perfect for those seeking animal comradery or a respite from the agonizing lonliness plauging the modern world. Want to bring your dog to work, but the boss won't allow it? The owner of a fiesty Betta has no such problem! Co-workers will be overjoyed by the fresh-air these underwater friends bring to a work environment. Check out this other site on Betta Fish for more details.

Get Started!

With the right tank, a bottle of dechlorinizer, some Betta food, and your Betta, you are ready to go!
